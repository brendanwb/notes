STAR Interview Answers

STAR: Situation

- Describing the situation that you were in that relates to the question or the task that you needed to accomplish.

- Offer a specific situation or event

- Detailed

- Can be from any area of life


STAR: Task

- What was the goal you were working towards?


STAR: Action

- Describe the actions taken to resolve the task

- Keep the focus on yourself and provide sufficient detail: What specific steps did you take OR how did you contribute to solving the problem

- Don't use phrases like "we did this" or "as part of a group"


STAR: Result

- Describe the results of your actions using specific and measurable statements that highlight the outcome of the situation

- Make sure you cover what happened as a result of your actions

- What you accomplished

- What you learned from that situation, that you can now apply to similar situations
